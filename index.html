Description

Shima is a note taking application that stands for simplicity and isolation.

The app's name, a Japanese word for island, is a nod towards Shima's functioning:

Your notes are saved solely on device, free of any network dependency.

Features

Classic Note Taking Abilities

Create, edit and delete your personal notes, without using the network.

Notes Are Organized In Categories

Notes are automatically divided into categories by how recently they've been created.

Pinning and unpinning notes to the top of the list is possible with a simple swipe.

User Friendly Design

Light and dark mode versions are available.

Haptic feedback assists for even easier navigation.
